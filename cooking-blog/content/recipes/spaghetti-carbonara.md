---
title: "Spaghetti Carbonara!"
date: 2025-04-13
description: "A classic Italian pasta dish."
ingredients:
  - 200g spaghetti
  - 2 eggs
  - 100g pancetta
  - 50g parmesan cheese
cook_time: "20 minutes"
---

## Instructions

1. Cook spaghetti.
2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
3. Beat eggs and mix with cheese.
4. Combine everything off heat.
